About Me

Hello, Alexander here!

As a recent graduate from Simon Fraser University with a degree in Computer Engineering, I’m passionate about diving into the world of operating systems, firmware, and embedded development. With strong proficiency in C and C++, I specialize in low-level programming that prioritizes efficiency and reliability, and my hands-on experience spans hardware design, from RTL to physical layout—providing me with a comprehensive understanding of the embedded systems stack.

In addition to C and C++ skills, I have a solid working knowledge of Python, used in my personal projects for tasks such as scripting, automation, and prototyping. Eager to expand my capabilities, I am committed to continually sharpening these secondary skills to support my core focus on embedded systems and firmware development.

Known as a quick learner who readily adapts to new challenges, I’m enthusiastic about contributing to projects that fuse innovation with practicality. My goal is to help drive technical progress where hardware and software intersect.

Bring on the future.
